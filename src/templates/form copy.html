This website is to track and help you with your sleep scedule

According to health organisations, sleep is one of the most important aspects of a human's health. Your brain completely functions depending on how much an individual sleeps within a day. physiological changes in sleep occur in the brain, when resting the brain automatically shuts down as if you have died for a certain period of time. This affects how we think and remember throughout the day. Sleep plays a vital role in good health and well-being throughout your life. The way you feel while you are awake depends in part on what happens while you are sleeping. During sleep, your body is working to support healthy brain function and maintain your physical health.
