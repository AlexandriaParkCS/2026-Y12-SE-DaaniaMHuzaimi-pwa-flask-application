✔️Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

What we collect

When you create an account, we collect your username and email address. When you log sleep, we collect your bedtime, wake time, quality rating, and optional notes. We only collect what is necessary to provide the service.

How we store it

Your password is never stored in plaintext. It is hashed using bcrypt with a unique salt before being saved. Your sleep data is stored in a local SQLite database and is never shared with third parties or sold to advertisers .

External services

SleepTracker displays a daily wellness quote from ZenQuotes API (zenquotes.io). This is a read-only anonymous request - no personal data is sent to ZenQuotes. If you prefer not to load external content, the app works fully without it .

Your rights

  • You can view all your data on the History page at any time .
  • You can edit or delete individual entries from the History page.
  • You can permanently delete your account and all data from the Sleep Goal page. This is immediate and irreversible.
